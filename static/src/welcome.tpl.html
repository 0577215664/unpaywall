You've installed Unpaywall!

We'll work in the background when you view research articles. If you see the green tab, click it to skip the paywall. if you see a gray tab it means we came up empty.

To get started, try this example article:

Information-Theoretic Approach to Quantum Error Correction and Reversible Measurement.

It's paywalled in the Proceedings of the Royal Society A, but Unpaywall finds a free copy uploaded by the authors to the CERN document server.

PS for the Open Access nerds: if you want to really dive into articles' OA status, the Unpaywall tab can change colors to indicate Green or Gold OA, too; enable it in settings.
